Learn More About Adelere Family Hospital

Adelere Family Hospital is a private hospital that offers a wide range of medical services to the residents of Ibadan and its environs. We provide quality healthcare services to our patients and we are committed to ensuring that our patients receive the best possible care. Our team of highly skilled and experienced healthcare professionals are dedicated to providing compassionate care to our patients. We offer a wide range of medical services including general medicine, pediatrics, obstetrics and gynecology, surgery, and more. At Adelere Family Hospital, we are committed to providing our patients with the highest quality of care in a warm and welcoming environment. We strive to make your experience with us as comfortable and stress-free as possible.

Explore services

Emergency Services

We provide emergency medical services to patients who require immediate medical attention. Our team of healthcare professionals are available 24/7 to provide emergency care to our patients.

Trauma Care

We provide trauma care services to patients who have suffered injuries as a result of accidents or other traumatic events. Our team of healthcare professionals are trained to provide care to patients with traumatic injuries.

Children care

We provide comprehensive healthcare services to children of all ages. Our team of pediatricians are dedicated to providing compassionate care to our young patients.

Maternal Care

Maternal health is of utmost importance to us. Our Obstetric and Gynaecological team are readily available.
