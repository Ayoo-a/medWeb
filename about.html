About Adelere Family Hospital

Adelere Family Hospital

Who We Are

We are a Healthcare team driven by Passion and Excellence, dispensing quality health care to our community. Our patient are our top priority. It is our desire for our patients to be well spirit, soul and body. We are an Ever-Learning, Ever-Evolving family, always seeking better ways to serve.

We Are Purpose-Driven

Our mission

To bring patient-centered healthcare to the Nigerian community, providing healthcare services with excellence and professionalism, especially in the aspects of:

Women Health Care

Trauma Care

Emergency Medicine & Health Education
